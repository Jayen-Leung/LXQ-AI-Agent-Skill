# Quick Direction Output Contract

Use with `light` intensity for early topic screening.

Return:

1. Known client/resource/budget boundary.
2. Three to five candidate directions, each with a title, question, client fit, feasibility, budget fit, main risk, and expected deliverable.
3. Recommended order based on the internal eight-dimension score.
4. At most three missing facts that could change the ranking.

Do not create a full QC report, evidence ledger, issue register, or complete grant unless requested. Do not expose a long scoring table unless the user asks, but preserve the reasoning.
