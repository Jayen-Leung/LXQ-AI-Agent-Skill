# Audit Report Output Contract

Use `strict` or `forensic` intensity.

Return:

1. Bounded outcome and reviewed scope.
2. Identity/provenance status.
3. Blockers and major issues with evidence.
4. Claim-to-evidence and cross-artifact consistency.
5. Required actions, owners, and verification.
6. Scientific decision and package readiness.
7. What remains unassessed.

Forensic intensity requires stronger source preservation, chain-of-custody description, raw-data/image provenance, and explicit separation of observed irregularity from misconduct conclusions.
