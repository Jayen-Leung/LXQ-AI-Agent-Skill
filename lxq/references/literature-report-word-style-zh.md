# 中文文献阅读报告 Word 版式规范

This profile preserves the output mode established by the LXQ FOXN3/NEK6 pulmonary-fibrosis reading report. It controls layout, not scientific content. Scientific claims remain governed by the literature QC and output contract.

## Page and running elements

- Paper: A4 portrait.
- Margins: top about 1.65 cm, bottom about 1.55 cm, left/right about 2.05 cm.
- Header: journal/year plus `文献阅读报告` at left, LXQ version at right, with a thin blue bottom rule.
- Footer: centered page number in the form `— 1 —`.
- No decorative cover page. Page 1 begins with the title and metadata.

## Typography

Preferred cross-platform font stack:

- English title: Times New Roman, 17 pt, bold, centered, dark charcoal.
- Chinese title: Microsoft YaHei / Noto Sans CJK SC, 14.5 pt, bold, centered, dark blue.
- Main section heading: Microsoft YaHei / Noto Sans CJK SC, 15 pt, bold, dark blue, preceded by a blue `▎` marker.
- Numbered result heading and secondary heading: 12.5 pt, bold, dark blue, preceded by `▎`.
- Body: Arial for Latin text and SimSun / Noto Serif CJK SC for Chinese, 10.5 pt, 1.45 line spacing, justified, first-line indent about 0.74 cm.
- Figure title: 9 pt, bold, centered.
- Evidence-location/source note: 8.5 pt, gray, centered.

Use fallbacks when the preferred CJK fonts are unavailable. Never package or distribute font files.

## Color system

- Primary dark blue: `#1F5A92`.
- Accent blue: `#2F75B5`.
- Light-blue fill: approximately `#EAF2F8`.
- Border blue: approximately `#8CB4D9`.
- Body charcoal: `#333333`.
- Secondary gray: `#666666`.

Do not add unrelated accent colors. The report should resemble a restrained journal-club or research-office handoff, not a promotional brochure.

## Tables and boxes

### Metadata table

- Five rows, two columns.
- Left column about 3.2 cm, bold dark-blue labels with light-blue fill.
- Right column about 13.2 cm, white or very pale-blue alternating fill.
- Thin blue borders and compact cell padding.

### Core conclusion box

- One-cell table with pale-blue fill and blue border.
- About 150-250 Chinese characters.
- Justified body text with normal paragraph rhythm.

### Evidence-chain matrix

- 2x2 matrix.
- Each cell begins with a numbered bold blue phrase, followed by a short evidence statement.

### Evidence-strength matrix

- Four columns: `证据环节 / LXQ支持等级 / 主要依据 / 边界`.
- Dark-blue header with white text.
- Pale alternating row fills.
- Support level centered; evidence and boundaries left aligned.

### Article-use table

- Three rows: `可直接支持 / 需要限定 / 不宜外推`.
- Left labels use pale-blue fill and bold blue text.

## Figures

- Place each figure near the first result paragraph that interprets it.
- Use a tight crop of the result figure or table, not a full page screenshot.
- Keep the crop large enough for labels to be legible.
- Do not distort aspect ratio.
- Under each figure add the Chinese figure title, then the evidence-location/source note.
- Disclose whether the visual is unchanged, cropped, scaled, redrawn, translated, or recombined.
- Avoid copying source article paragraphs into the figure area.

## Page-flow controls

- Keep section headings with the following paragraph.
- Avoid leaving a heading or figure caption alone at the bottom of a page.
- Keep a figure, its Chinese title, and its evidence-location note together where feasible.
- Split long evidence tables only when necessary and repeat the header row.
- Do not force every major section to a new page; preserve a continuous reading flow.

## Naming

Recommended file names:

- `LXQ_[short-paper-key]_文献阅读报告.docx`
- `LXQ_[short-paper-key]_文献阅读报告.pdf`

The short key should use the central gene/pathway, disease, method, or author-year identifier and avoid protected personal information.
